Cases

While nothing we do is meant to be kept from the public, we don't always publish details on our involvement in the CBR incidents and pharmaceutical disasters that we respond to. However, on occasion, we do release documentation that details a specific instance for the sake of educating the public. This is especially important in times where the media may not report honestly on a situation or where they might not have known any concrete details at all.

Terragrigia Panic

The Terragrigia Panic was the name that was given by the media with regards to a bioterrorist attack caused by Il Veltro, which was responsible for releasing the T-Abyss virus on the floating city of Terragrigia in 2004.
