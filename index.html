FOR A FUTURE WITHOUT FEAR

We're fighting day and night to eliminate bioterrorism.

The world deserves better.

TerraSave International strives to eliminate biological, chemical and radiological terrorism to create a future free from the fear and devastation wrought by these disasters.

We're going to make it happen.

With help from volunteers and local collective action all around the globe, we work tirelessly to organize protests and workshops to spark legislative change and pharmaceutical regulation.

Here's how you can help.

The most important thing the average person can do is vote! Our efforts constantly bring new measures to the table and the only way we can get them passed is with the help of people like you.

Want a more active role? We have regional headquarter offices all over the world and branches in many localities. Visit one or check out our join us page for more information on how you can contribute to the cause.
