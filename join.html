Come help us in the fight for a future without fear.

Bioterrorism is evolving quickly. New viruses and variants are engineered at alarming rates, casting a shadow of uncertainty and fear over our futures. Corporations and governments turn a blind eye to it for power and profit. Worse still, some governments have come out in active support of its use. And perhaps the worst of all, there are still civilians and politicians in first-world countries like the United States who believe the rampant bioterrorism that has plagued us since the tragedy in Raccoon City, Michigan is all a hoax to push a radical agenda. This is where we come in.

As dire as things may seem, the world isn't without hope. TerraSave is fighting every day to eliminate bioterrorism. By partnering closely with the BSAA to monitor trends and deploy agents to investigate suspicious activities and counter bioterror attacks, organizing protests against sponsors of bioterrorism, coordinating education programs for communities and schools, advocating for legislative change, and much more, we are working tireslessly to safeguard our future.

We are 500+ strong and our reach spans over 100 countries worldwide. No matter where you're located, we're always looking for fresh faces to join the cause in whatever capacity you think fits for you.
