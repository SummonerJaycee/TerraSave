Regional Offices & Facilities

Terrasave International has regional offices in five key location all around the world. Each regional facility is equipped with the same state-of-the-art technology and programs needed to fight against CBR terrorism and pharmaceutical disaters, to provide unparalled care for our wards and to offer medical assistance as needed.

While these regional offices are our main facilities in these locations, every regional office has several branches in different locales all over their jurisdiction. These local branches are smaller and aren't as well equipped, but are capable of providing basic services and transporting civilians and TerraSave personnel to and from the main regional office.

North America Toronto, Ontario.

Toronto, Ontario. This is our main, central headquarters. It's located in downtown Toronto next to the BSAA North American HQ building.

Europe Milan, Italy.

Milan, Italy. Our European headquarters is located in the Porta Nuova business district in Milan, near the gorgeous Vertical Forest.

Asia Seoul, South Korea.

Seoul, South Korea. Our Asian headquarters is located in the Seocho District of Seoul, a short distance from the Han River.

West Africa Lagos, Nigeria.

Lagos, Nigeria. Our West African headquarters is located near the Murtala Muhammed International Airport on mainland Lagos.

Oceania Sydney, Australia.

Sydney, Australia. Our Oceanic headquarters is located in The Rocks, near the Museum of Contempary Art and with an incredible view of the Sydney Opera House across The Habour.
