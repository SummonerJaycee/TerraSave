Bioterrorism. It strikes fear in the hearts of everyone.

Viruses have been the cause of tragic accidents and are exploited to cause horrific disasters all over the globe. And who can you trust? Politicians who will sell their souls for votes? Corporations who betray consumers for a quick buck? No, but you can count on us instead.

We're above the politics and we're not afraid of those in power. We're always watching and ready to face the threat head-on.

We are Terrasave. To save the world from fear, let's take action together.

Our responsibilities are extensive.

Our battle against CBR terrorism and Big Pharma is far-reaching and includes more than just protests and workshops aimed at pushing change and educating the public.

In times of disaster, we're always ready.

Emergency Assistance

We provide aid in the form of supplies and manpower during and after the incident, including medical assistance to victims.

Search & Rescue

We lead and conduct search and rescue efforts, sparing to expense to make sure we bring as many people home safe as we can.

Ensuring Justice

When parties authorized to make arrests are present, our agents assists in any way they can to ensure the persons responsible for these tragedies face the consequences.

Nurturing the Most Vulnerable

Children orphaned by CBR and pharmaceutical disasters are taken in as our wards, where we strive to provide them with the best care available and help them find a brighter future.

But our work doesn't stop there.

Outside of horrific disaster situations, we work hard to ensure that they don't happen in the first place.

  • We monitor the distrubition of medication.
  • We ensure safe drug development.
  • We ensure the safety of research facilities.
  • We organize mass protests against corporations and governments.
  • We conduct victim outreach.
  • We enlighten the public about CBR threats and pharmaceutical dangers.
  • We whistle-blow governments and corporations who conceal CBR terrorism and drug-induced suffering.
  • We investigate suspected B.O.W activity and relay our findings to the BSAA as needed.
  • We observe and report on CBR terrorism trends around the globe.
  • We organize and participate in fundraising efforts for our organization, affiliate organizations and related start-ups.

To save the world from fear, let's take action together.
